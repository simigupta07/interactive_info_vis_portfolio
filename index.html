Analog Alarm Clock

Context: This clock visualizes an alarm clock that is shown in analog style vs digital style. Most alarm clocks I have seen are digital clocks so I wanted to make an analog clock.

  • Context: This clock is used for at home use or in office. I have an alarm clock next to my bedside table.
  • Design decisions: Key choices that I have made is adding hour and minute hand be the same color and distinguishing that with the seconds hand so it is readable. I also added a stand where the clock would sit on to resemble a sitting clock.
  • Future work: I want to add a chime sound when the alarm goes off however I was having trouble figuring out what sound to include.

Apple Watch Clock

Context: This clock is an apple watch which shows the time, and then rings for progress. Many people have this to show their fitness goal rings.

  • Context: This is used for activity & just to check the time on the go.
  • Design decisions: I made sure to use different colors for the ring, and one of the rings fills based on the time. The time is also shown in the center.
  • Future work: I want to label what each ring showcases, or show logos/images to describe what each ring is about. I want each ring to show activities too besides time.

Digital Alarm Clock

Context: This sketch shows a digital alarm clock that is used on a bedside table. I was trying to resemble what my digital alarm clock would look like.

  • Context: This is used on the bedside table at home or in office. It shows the time and there is a red light that is blinking.
  • Design decisions: There is a box style digital clock that shows the time. It is simple yet readable. It also shows a bar on the top with a red light that blinks. The light is red.
  • Future work: I wish I could make the light change colors or have the alarm signal a sound when it goes off.
